Our Heritage

Founded in 1980, Al Fareed Zarri House became a symbol of excellence in traditional Zarri craftsmanship, creating unique bridal dresses, groom attire, and elegant Zarri designs. Under the visionary leadership of Mian Muhammad Saeed Sidhu, the foundation of quality, precision, and royal artistry was established. Alongside him, his son Muhammad Ahsan Saeed (Late) played a vital role in strengthening and preserving the heritage of fine craftsmanship, ensuring that the legacy continued with dedication and passion. Today, the responsibility of Al Fareed Zarri House has been proudly handed over to Muhammad Mohsin Saeed and Muhammad Sufyan Saeed who continue this proud legacy by blending traditional techniques with modern elegance — serving generations with timeless, handcrafted luxury.

Each thread we weave tells a story of royalty, patience, and unparalleled skill.
